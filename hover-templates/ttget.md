<span style='color:var(--vscode-symbolIcon-methodForeground);'>ttget</span> (<span style='color:var(--vscode-symbolIcon-variableForeground);'>TNUM SEL</span>) 

Get tasmota timer setting from timer TNUM (1 .. 16)

SEL:
- 0 = time
- 1 = time window
- 2 = repeat
- 3 = days
- 4 = device
- 5 = power
- 6 = mode
- 7 = arm
